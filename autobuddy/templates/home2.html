Welcome to AutoBuddy

Want to know if the mechanic is giving you a fair price? AutoBuddy has you covered for that too! Through the use of two tools, the Cost Finder and Is It Fair? tool, you can make sure that you get the help that you need to save yourself from swindlers at the shop and net yourself some extra cash from savings.

If you would like to learn more about these tools, scroll down and read on the sections below for each tool.

Cost Finder Auto Repair Estimator

Ever heard a strange noise coming from your car and wondered how much it would cost to have the mechanic take a look and fix it? Now you can with AutoBuddy's Cost Finder tool! Cost Finder works by taking the information about your car and repair, and adding up the total amount paid for by users with the same car and repair(s). Afterwards, this sum is then divided by the number of the reports submitted of this car and repair(s), just like finding the average. This average is then shown to you, giving you the average price of your repairs paid for by real people.

Is it Fair? Quote Comparison Tool

We've all heard of the stories of people coming into the shop, being told of repairs that need to be done, forking over ludicrous amounts of money, and find out that the repairs not only weren't need, but were way overcharged. While AutoBuddy's Is It Fair? tool won't help you avoid falling victim to unnecessary repairs, it can help you avoid paying too much at the shop.

The Is It Fair? tool takes in your vehicle's information, such as its year, make, and model, along with the repairs you did and how much it cost. AutoBuddy will then compare the price you paid to the average price paid for by users who paid for the same repairs on the same vehicle. Based on how far below or above your price is compared to the average price calculated by AutoBuddy, you will be notified whether you are getting swindled or are catching a lucky break at the shop.

Cost Finder Estimator Tool

You've reached the Cost Finder tool, where you can get an estimated cost the a set of repairs you plan to do for your car. Using the Cost Finder tool is very simple, all you have to do is provide AutoBuddy with three details about your car, its year, its make, and its model, and the set of repairs that you plan to do. Once you typed in that information, simply click the submit button, and AutoBuddy will pull up the price you'll likely pay faster than you can say "Hakuna Matata".

Is It Fair? Quote Comparison Tool

As mentioned before, the Is It Fair? tool allows you to check if the price you were given from your mechanic is fair and reasonable. Like Cost Finder, the Is It Fair? tool takes in your vehicle's year, make, and model, along with the repairs and the price you were quoted for them. This tool then compares your price with the average price of the repairs calculated by AutoBuddy, and determines whether your price is fair based on whether it is cheaper or more expensive.

If your price is significantly below the calculated average, the difference will be highlighted in dark green, signifying that you are one lucky individual.

If your price is below the calculated average, the difference will be highlighted in green, signifying that you found a good deal and (hopefully) a good mechanic.

If your price is either at the calculated average or slightly above it, the difference will be highlighted in yellow, which is certainly not the end of the world, but doesn't call for a celebratory jump.

If your price is significantly above the calculated average, the difference will be highlighted in red, which means that you're probably getting overcharged and might want to shop around for another mechanic.

Contribute to AutoBuddy!
