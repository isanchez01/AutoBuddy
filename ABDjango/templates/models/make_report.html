Help AutoBuddy by Submitting Your Repair Bill

AutoBuddy is powered by the reports that users submit to AutoBuddy. These reports include the user's vehicle information (year, make, and model), which repairs were done on their car, the cost of these repairs, and their name. The names are not collected by AutoBuddy and are simply used as references for the databases behind the scenes. All entries in a report are sent to AutoBuddy, which then stores them in the database.

The AutoBuddy Estimator and Is It Fair? tools take the car and repair information you submitted, and searches through the database for all entries that match your car and repair(s). AutoBuddy then calculates the sum and average of these entries, and returns the result back to you.
